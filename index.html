Student Bio

Ashley Edds

NC born and bred! I grew up in Henderson NC and attended UNC from 2007-2011. I graduated with a dual degree in Communications Studies (with a focus in Speech and Hearing) and Linguistics. I then attended ECU and graduated with a Master's in Communication Sciences and Disorders.


Since graduation, I have completed a Clinical Fellowship at Rex Hopsital and have been working at Duke University Hospital for three years. I work primarily with adults in our inpatient Cardiothroacic Surgical Unit and enjoy working with lung transplant patients most. I help people who are unable to swallow safely or talk after surgery due to tracheostomy tubes.


In my free time, I enjoy cooking, going to Orange Theory Fitness, exploring local restaurants, and hiking. I also really enjoy travelling. In 2017, I have visited 5 different countries and have spent time in LA and Cape Cod. I have been married for 4 years and have two very lovable cats, Charlie and Valentine.
